WELCOME TO VESIT

OUR MOTTO

  1. To be a pioneering center of excellence in engineering education, fostering innovation, leadership, and holistic development.
  2. We envision our engineering college as a transformative hub that nurtures and empowers the brightest minds to thrive in a rapidly evolving world.
  3. Through cutting-edge curricula, state-of-the-art facilities, and a dedicated faculty, we aim to provide an inspiring learning environment that instills a passion for discovery and problem-solving.
  4. Our vision is to equip our students with a profound understanding of engineering principles while encouraging creativity, collaboration, and ethical responsibility.
  5. As an institution, we strive to be at the forefront of research and technology, driving innovation and breakthroughs that positively impact society.

OUR PRINCIPAL

Shri.B.L.Boolani

VES College was established in 1984 as an initiative of our senior most trustee and President of VES, Shri. B. L. Boolani ji. He with the helping hands of Prof. Jharana Das madam, our chief co-ordinator and late Prof. J.K.Lalla, former Principal of KMK College of Pharmacy established our College in 1984.
